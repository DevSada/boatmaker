Deatil Description

FBavaria 34 gives you the impossible. She is a small boat on the outside with large volume cabins on the inside. Kitchen is small but gives you everything you need, a stove, a fridge and a decent size sink. Salon seat can be converted into another berth so you can bring one more friend with you on your sailing cruise trough Croatia. Two steering wheels on the boat of this size significantly opens up a cockpit and allows easy access to swimming platform. She will sail gracefully on afternoon mistral with you at it's helm while the crew comfortably sits in the cockpit. A large screen chart plotter in the cockpit connected to the autopilot will guide you to all the ports and bays of your memorable holiday.
